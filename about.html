About This Little Cafe

At This Little Cafe, we believe everyone deserves a delicious cup of coffee made from top-quality beans. We use fresh coffee from Velvet Sunrise Coffee Roasters in Stouffville, Ontario, and pair it with fresh baked goods. Reopening inside the Magna Centre in Newmarket, we bring a fresh new look and exciting products while continuing our tradition since 2010. Our focus is supporting local businesses and serving our community with coffee, pastries, and specialty drinks made just for you.
